About BreastCancerAI

Empowering Health Through AI Technology

BreastCancerAI is an innovative web-based system that leverages machine learning to provide early risk assessment for breast cancer, making health awareness accessible to everyone.

Our Vision

To create a world where every individual has access to early breast cancer risk awareness, empowering people to take proactive steps toward their health regardless of their location or economic status.

Our Mission

To provide a free, accessible, and easy-to-use AI-powered tool that helps individuals assess their breast cancer risk using simple health information, encouraging early detection and timely medical consultation.

Our Strategic Goals

Increase Awareness

Educate users about breast cancer symptoms, risk factors, and the importance of early detection.

Provide Accurate Predictions

Deliver reliable risk assessments using a validated Logistic Regression model with 97% accuracy.

Ensure Accessibility

Make the system available on all devices with a responsive, user-friendly interface.

Protect Privacy

Safeguard user data with encrypted passwords and secure database management.

Support Education

Serve as a valuable resource for students, researchers, and healthcare professionals.

Promote Prevention

Encourage healthy lifestyle choices through personalized recommendations and health tips.

Objectives

ML-Based Prediction Model

Create a model based on machine learning techniques using Logistic Regression for accurate binary classification of breast cancer risk.

Enhance Diagnosis Quality

Contribute towards enhancing the quality of diagnosis and early treatment through accurate risk assessment.

Improve Healthcare Systems

Reduce manual time and efforts put into diagnosis through AI assistance and automated risk evaluation.

Suggested Solution

The proposed solution is to develop a machine learning–based prediction model that analyzes simple user inputs such as age, BMI, and basic health information to estimate the likelihood of breast cancer. The system is implemented as an easy-to-use web application that allows users to enter their data and receive an instant risk level. The goal of the solution is to support early awareness, reduce delays in medical consultation, and offer a more accessible tool compared to traditional tests that require medical imaging or laboratory procedures.

Project Scope

  • Complete machine learning pipeline (preprocessing, training, evaluation)
  • Functional web interface with user registration and authentication
  • Secure data handling and prediction results storage
  • Personalized feedback and recommendations based on risk level
  • Does NOT replace professional medical diagnosis
  • No advanced clinical imaging or biopsy analysis
  • No integration with hospital databases
